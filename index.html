Problem

Traditional smoke detectors react only after smoke appears, creating dangerous delays. Ember aims to detect ignition before smoke, minimizing response time and risk.

Solution

By merging solar-blind UV sensing with computer vision, Ember identifies flame signatures instantly—even in bright environments—offering a new standard of fire awareness.

Technology

Ember’s dual-sensor architecture fuses ultraviolet wake logic with onboard AI. It distinguishes dangerous ignition events from safe, supervised flames such as candles or stoves.

Value Proposition

Ember enables earlier detection, fewer false alarms, and superior reliability—all while consuming minimal power for extended operation.

Market

With over 340,000 home fires annually in the U.S., the global fire detection market is growing beyond $80 billion. Ember’s intelligent detection brings a modern solution to a century-old problem.
