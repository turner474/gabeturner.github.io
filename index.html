Problem

Most smoke alarms only respond once smoke fills the air, often too late. Ember detects ignition right away, giving people more time to act and stay safe.

Solution

Ember uses paired sensors and onboard processing to recognize flames instantly, even in bright or changing light. It can tell the difference between a real fire and safe light sources like candles or stovetops.

Technology

Ember utilizes wake-interrupt logic to continuously monitor ultraviolet activity, activating only when distinct flame signatures appear, ensuring quick response with minimal power use. When a potential ignition is detected, it “wakes” to analyze and confirm before triggering an alert — saving energy while staying responsive.

Value Proposition

Ember enables earlier detection, saving lives and property with superior reliability.

Market

Home fires affect hundreds of thousands of families every year. Ember brings a modern, practical approach to a problem that hasn’t evolved in decades.
